## Warning

Public download availability supports local use and private analysis. It does
not by itself mean the PDF files, copied pages, tables, screenshots, or long
verbatim examples should be committed, packaged, or redistributed with this
plugin. Keep derived guidance original and concise unless a license review
confirms broader reuse.
