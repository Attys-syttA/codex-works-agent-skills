# Editorial Checklist

## Before editing

- Identify the audience and requested mode.
- Mark protected spans: quotes, code, logs, paths, identifiers, legal text, numbers, dates.
- List the facts and obligations that must remain unchanged.
- Check tegezés or magázás.

## During editing

- Put the main point early.
- Keep one main action or finding per sentence.
- Use specific verbs.
- Keep necessary terms; remove decorative wording.
- Preserve risk strength, uncertainty, and responsibility.
- Avoid repeated template phrases unless the text is intentionally tabular.

## Final pass

- Compare the rewritten text with the protected spans.
- Check grammar, case endings, word order, and punctuation.
- Check whether the selected mode stayed consistent.
- Remove AI-like filler.
- Confirm that no new fact, promise, deadline, source, or legal effect appeared.
